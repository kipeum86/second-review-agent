# Legal Source URLs by Jurisdiction

Primary legal databases for citation verification. Use these as authoritative sources.

---

## Korean (KR)

| Source | URL | Content |
|--------|-----|---------|
| **법제처 국가법령정보센터** | `law.go.kr` | All Korean statutes (법률), presidential decrees (시행령), ministerial regulations (시행규칙) |
| **대법원 종합법률정보** | `glaw.scourt.go.kr` | Korean case law (대법원, 고등법원, 지방법원 판결) |
| **국회 의안정보시스템** | `likms.assembly.go.kr/bill` | Legislative history, pending bills |
| **헌법재판소** | `ccourt.go.kr` | Constitutional Court decisions |
| **법제처 행정규칙** | `law.go.kr/행정규칙` | Administrative rules, guidelines, notifications |

### Korean Search Tips
- Statute search: `법령명` field on law.go.kr is most reliable
- Case search: Use full case number format `YYYY[type]NNNNN`
- 시행령 vs 법률: Always verify the correct level (법률 ≠ 시행령 ≠ 시행규칙)
- Repealed laws: law.go.kr shows repeal status — check 시행일 and 폐지일

## United States (US)

| Source | URL | Content |
|--------|-----|---------|
| **US Code (Congress.gov)** | `congress.gov` | Federal statutes |
| **US Code (House)** | `uscode.house.gov` | Federal statutes (alternative) |
| **eCFR** | `ecfr.gov` | Code of Federal Regulations (current) |
| **Federal Register** | `federalregister.gov` | Proposed and final rules |
| **CourtListener** | `courtlistener.com` | Federal and state case law (free) |
| **Google Scholar** | `scholar.google.com` | Case law search |
| **SEC EDGAR** | `sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar` | Securities filings and regulations |

### US Search Tips
- USC: Use title + section format: `42 U.S.C. § 1983`
- CFR: Use title + part + section: `17 C.F.R. § 240.10b-5`
- Cases: Reporter citation is most precise: `410 U.S. 113`
- Check amendment status: USC is codified — check for amendments since cited version

## European Union (EU)

| Source | URL | Content |
|--------|-----|---------|
| **EUR-Lex** | `eur-lex.europa.eu` | EU legislation, case law, treaties |
| **CURIA** | `curia.europa.eu` | Court of Justice of the EU decisions |
| **EBA** | `eba.europa.eu` | European Banking Authority guidelines |
| **ESMA** | `esma.europa.eu` | European Securities and Markets Authority |

### EU Search Tips
- CELEX number is the most precise identifier for EUR-Lex
- Regulation numbering: `(EU) 2016/679` (GDPR) — year/number format
- Directive numbering: `2006/123/EC` — year/number/jurisdiction
- Check consolidated versions on EUR-Lex for amendments

---

## Verification Priority

When multiple sources are available:
1. **Official government databases** (law.go.kr, congress.gov, eur-lex) — highest authority
2. **Court databases** (glaw.scourt.go.kr, courtlistener.com, curia.europa.eu)
3. **Secondary legal databases** (scholar.google.com, legal commentaries)
4. **General web search** — lowest authority, use only as last resort

# Legal Register Guide — Korean & English

Requirements for formal register in legal documents.

---

## Korean (한국어) Legal Register

### Required Register by Document Type

| Document Type | Register | Ending Style |
|--------------|----------|--------------|
| 법률의견서 | 문어체 격식 | ~합니다/~입니다 또는 ~이다/~한다 |
| 소장/준비서면 | 문어체 격식 | ~이다/~한다 (법원 제출용) |
| 이사회 결의서 | 문어체 격식 | ~이다/~한다 |
| 내부 메모 | 경어체 가능 | ~합니다/~입니다 |
| 계약서 | 문어체 격식 | ~이다/~한다 |
| 리서치 리포트 | 문어체 격식 | ~이다/~한다 또는 ~합니다 |

### Formal Register Rules (문어체)

1. **종결어미 일관성**: 한 문서 내에서 ~이다체와 ~합니다체를 혼용하지 않음
2. **구어체 금지**: 해요체, 반말 종결어미 금지 (해, 해요, 거든, 잖아)
3. **경어 수준**: 법원 제출 문서는 ~이다체, 의뢰인 향 문서는 ~합니다체 가능
4. **접속부사**: 그리고→또한/아울러, 그런데→다만/그러나, 그래서→따라서/이에
5. **지시어**: 이것→이, 저것→해당, 여기→본건/당해
6. **인칭**: 나→본인/당사자, 우리→당사/당법인

### Common Errors

| Error | Correction | Severity |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| 해요체 in formal doc | → 합니다체 or 이다체 | Major |
| 반말 혼입 (해, 됨) | → 격식체 종결 | Major |
| 구어체 접속사 (그리고, 그래서) | → 격식 접속 (또한, 따라서) | Minor |
| 종결어미 혼용 | → 일관된 체제 유지 | Minor |
| 불필요한 존칭 | → 적절한 수준 조정 | Suggestion |

## English Legal Register

### Required Register by Document Type

| Document Type | Register | Tone |
|--------------|----------|------|
| Legal opinion | Formal professional | Measured, authoritative |
| Court filing | Formal judicial | Respectful to court, precise |
| Board resolution | Formal corporate | Declarative, concise |
| Internal memo | Professional | Direct, may be less formal |
| Contract | Formal contractual | Precise, unambiguous |
| Research report | Formal academic | Analytical, cited |

### Plain Language Principles

1. **Prefer active voice**: "The Company shall deliver" not "delivery shall be made by the Company"
2. **Avoid archaic terms** unless standard in jurisdiction:
   - ❌ witnesseth, hereinbefore, aforesaid, said (as adjective)
   - ✅ this agreement provides, above, the [specific noun]
3. **One idea per sentence**: Break compound-complex sentences
4. **Define terms on first use**: Don't assume reader knows abbreviations
5. **Use "must" for obligations**: "shall" is declining in modern drafting

### Formality Markers

| Too Informal | Appropriate | Too Archaic |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| don't, can't | do not, cannot | doth, witnesseth |
| OK, okay | acceptable | agreeable (in this context) |
| a lot of | significant, substantial | a great many |
| get, got | obtain, receive | procure |
| big | significant, material | considerable |

### Citation Form

| Jurisdiction | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| US Federal | Bluebook (20th ed.) |
| US State | Local court rules + Bluebook |
| UK/Commonwealth | OSCOLA (4th ed.) |
| EU | Official Journal citation format |
| International | ILM / ICJ Reports |

## Cross-Language Consistency

When a document references translations:
- Flag material divergences between Korean and English versions
- Note which version controls (typically specified in the contract)
- Ensure legal terms are translated consistently throughout
